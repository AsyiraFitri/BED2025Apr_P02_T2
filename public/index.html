Welcome to EverydayCare!

A trusted companion for seniors to live independently, confidently, and meaningfully every day.

Whether you're getting around town, keeping up with your health, joining a hobby group, or just need a little help — EverydayCare brings it all together in one easy-to-use place. Simple tools, clear guidance, and friendly support, made with you in mind.

Navigation & Transport Assistant

Travel made easier, just for you.
Quickly save places you visit often and add simple notes to remember why they matter. Check real-time bus with just a bus-stop code, and explore nearby parks, clinics, markets, and more. When it’s time to go, get clear step-by-step directions, helpful landmarks, and even see what the area looks like ahead of time using Street View. Everything you need, all in one easy place.

Clear directions that guide you with confidence—featuring step-by-step instructions, helpful landmarks, live traffic updates, and built-in Street View. You can even drop the 'little yellow figure' onto the map to see what the area looks like before you go.
